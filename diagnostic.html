Sales Engine Diagnostic

Most sales teams are working hard, but not all are operating within a structured system that allows them to consistently perform. This short diagnostic helps you assess the strength of your current sales engine across sales process, sales team behaviour, sales management rhythm, and leadership visibility.

Takes around 2 minutes to complete

What this diagnostic helps reveal

Inconsistent revenue performance is usually not caused by a lack of effort. More often it comes from unclear process, weak management rhythm, uneven accountability, and limited visibility of what good performance should look like.

What happens next

You complete the 12 questions
You receive your Sales Engine score
You see which areas are strongest and weakest
You can choose to discuss the result with SaleSycle

Sales Process

Assess how clearly opportunities move and how reliably the commercial process is being followed.

1. Do your salespeople follow a clearly defined and consistent sales process when progressing opportunities?
2. Is discovery with customers structured and designed to uncover commercial impact rather than just product needs?
3. Do opportunities progress through your pipeline based on agreed commercial commitments rather than optimistic forecasting?

Sales Team Behaviour

Assess whether the sales team is working with consistency, preparation, and stronger commercial conversations.

4. Do salespeople consistently prepare for customer meetings with a clear objective and structured questioning approach?
5. Are sales conversations focused on understanding the customer’s business challenges rather than presenting product features?
6. Does your team confidently build and present business cases that justify investment from the customer perspective?

Sales Management Rhythm

Assess whether managers are reinforcing the system in a visible, practical, and commercially useful way.

7. Do sales managers run structured pipeline reviews that focus on deal quality rather than just deal size?
8. Are sales managers actively coaching salespeople on live opportunities rather than primarily reviewing activity numbers?
9. Is there a consistent rhythm of performance conversations between managers and the sales team?

Leadership Visibility

Assess whether senior leadership is clearly backing the commercial system and reinforcing standards.

10. Does senior leadership clearly communicate what good sales performance looks like within the organisation?
11. Are commercial expectations and performance standards visible across the sales organisation?
12. Do leaders actively reinforce the behaviours and disciplines that drive long term revenue performance?
