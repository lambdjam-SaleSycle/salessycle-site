The AIDE Performance Framework

Predictable revenue is not created through isolated training days or short term motivational initiatives.

It is built through aligned leadership, empowered sales managers and accountable sales teams operating within a clear, structured performance system.

AIDE is our four pillar methodology designed to align the top, equip the middle and elevate the front line where revenue is ultimately won, strengthened and sustained.

A

Assess

We immerse ourselves in your commercial reality, analysing pipeline strength, process clarity and behavioural patterns across leadership and sales teams.

Clarity replaces assumption. Opportunity becomes visible.

I

Integrate

Leaders and managers align first, establishing expectations and performance standards across the organisation.

We then cascade into the sales team with mentorship and structured support so alignment becomes action, not theory.

D

Develop

Sales teams practise in real selling environments while managers coach with consistency and intent.

Behaviour shifts from activity tracking to genuine performance ownership, driving measurable improvement.

E

Elevate

With behaviours stabilised and confidence growing across the sales team, performance begins to compound.

Revenue becomes predictable, momentum builds and the cycle sustains itself.
